My Approach

I get to know and understand the user, see how they’re using the product currently and develop one or more personae based on my observations. I identify pain points and layout the information architecture of possible solutions using flow charts, and static wires. Then I prototype the top concepts, evaluate, and repeat, until I believe that a design is ready for implementation. But of course, you never really finish a design...you just stop working on it.
