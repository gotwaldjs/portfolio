    • PROJECT OVERVIEW

    The challenge with monitoring social channels during real-time events is being able to digest information across multiple social channels efficiently, and curating a meaningful response. Our end goal was to create a platform that allowed content managers to monitor the conversation around our sponsored real-time events efficentialy while fully grasping the conversation around it. This would allow the content manager to create more impactful responses, and messages addded to the conversation.

    PROCESS

    First things first, how would the metrics be broken up? What buckets would certain things fall into? What buckets were the most important? In short, data is kinda like a natural resource in a lot of ways. We are always looking for where, and what new information might be mined. Simplest way to figure this out is to start asking asking questions with answering them in a manner that ultimately came down to what would be the highest prioty for the end user.
